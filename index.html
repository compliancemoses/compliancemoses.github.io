Common Questions

What companies ask
before engaging us

My supplier gave me a COA showing our lead levels. Isn't that enough for Prop 65?
No. A supplier Certificate of Analysis measures lead in parts per million — that is a concentration measurement. California Prop 65 requires a Maximum Allowable Dose Level calculation — a micrograms-per-day exposure figure based on how a person actually uses or consumes your product. These are two different measurements and two different standards. A Chinese factory COA showing 0.3 ppm lead does not tell you whether daily exposure from your product exceeds the 0.5 microgram per day MADL threshold. Only an ISO 17025 accredited laboratory conducting a product-specific exposure analysis can produce that number. Without it you have no documented technical defense.
My customs broker is handling my FDA detention. What else do I need?
Your customs broker handles the CBP entry side — the tariff classification, the entry filing, the bond. That is their role and they do it well. What they cannot do is prepare the FDA admissibility response. FDA and CBP are two separate agencies with two separate processes. The FDA admissibility package — corrected labeling to 21 CFR standards, reconditioning application, private laboratory analysis, formal correspondence to the FDA District Office — requires a compliance specialist, not a customs broker. Moses Compliance handles the FDA side simultaneously with your broker covering CBP. Both agencies need responses. Only one of them is being addressed if you are working with a broker alone.
Do I need to fire my lawyer to work with Moses Compliance?
No. Moses Compliance works alongside your legal counsel, not instead of them. Your attorney handles the legal response and negotiation. We handle the technical documentation layer — the lab testing, the MADL calculations, the corrected labeling, the corrective action evidence. This is the technical file your attorney needs to negotiate from strength rather than settle blind. We coordinate directly with your legal team and deliver the documentation they need in the format they need it.
We received a Prop 65 notice 4 months ago and did nothing. Is it too late?
It is not too late but you are in a more exposed position than you were at day 60. The 60-day window for the plaintiff to file a lawsuit has passed, which means they have standing to file a formal summons today without further notice. The statute of limitations for Prop 65 penalties runs one year from the violation date and three years for injunctive relief. The notice on the California AG database is also visible to every retail buyer, distributor, and Amazon compliance team running vendor due diligence. Moses Compliance can prepare the MADL documentation and safe harbor evidence that closes the technical gap regardless of where you are in the timeline. Earlier is better but later is still better than never.
What labs do you work with and how does lab coordination work?
We coordinate with ISO 17025 accredited laboratories that are accepted by CPSC, FDA, and California OEHHA for the specific testing your situation requires. We identify the correct facility for your chemical profile — a lab accredited for dietary supplement heavy metals testing is different from one accredited for DEHP phthalates in consumer products. We manage sample preparation and submission, the testing timeline, result review, and incorporation of findings into your compliance documentation. You do not interact with the laboratory directly. We manage the entire process and deliver the completed documentation package.
Can you help if my Amazon listing was suppressed for a compliance reason?
Yes. Amazon compliance suppressions typically require specific documentation — a Certificate of Conformity, Children's Product Certificate, TIC verification for supplements, or corrected labeling that matches the physical product. Moses Compliance identifies the exact documentation Amazon requires for your specific ASIN, coordinates the lab testing or documentation preparation, and prepares the submission package for Amazon's Manage Your Compliance dashboard. For EU Safety Gate listings that are also causing Amazon EU suppression we handle both the Safety Gate defense documentation and the Amazon EU reinstatement package simultaneously.
