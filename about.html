What We Actually Do

When a company receives an FDA import refusal, a Prop 65 60-day notice, an EU Safety Gate listing, or an Amazon compliance action — the clock starts immediately. Most businesses do not know what to do. Their customs broker handles the logistics side. Their lawyer handles the legal side. But the technical compliance gap — the documentation, the lab testing, the corrective action evidence, the regulatory filings — is where companies lose.

Moses Compliance fills that gap. We prepare the technical documentation your legal team needs to defend you. We coordinate with accredited laboratories. We manage the regulatory correspondence. We work alongside your existing counsel and logistics providers — completing the picture, not replacing it.

We monitor FDA refusal reports, CPSC recalls, Prop 65 filings, EU Safety Gate notifications, and Amazon enforcement actions daily. We find violations the moment they happen. We reach out to affected businesses before they have had time to panic.

The Compliance Gap Nobody Fills

There is a gap between what lawyers handle and what logistics providers handle. A Prop 65 notice requires legal defense — and it also requires safe harbor threshold testing, exposure assessment documentation, and warning label compliance. Your attorney handles the courtroom. Moses Compliance handles the laboratory.

An FDA import refusal requires a customs entry response — and it also requires a formal admissibility package, corrected labeling documentation, and correspondence with the FDA District Office. Your customs broker handles CBP. Moses Compliance handles FDA.

An EU Safety Gate listing requires market withdrawal — and it also requires a corrective action defense, EU Responsible Person representation, and Amazon EU reinstatement documentation. Nobody else covers all three simultaneously. Moses Compliance does.

Service Details

What each engagement includes

Prop 65 Defense

California Prop 65 — Notice Response & Post-Settlement Compliance

For companies that have received a 60-day Prop 65 notice: Moses Compliance prepares the technical compliance layer that runs alongside your legal defense — safe harbor threshold testing through accredited California-recognized laboratories, exposure assessment documentation, warning label design and implementation, and corrective action evidence package for your attorney. For companies that have already settled: our post-settlement compliance audit reviews your full catalog against Prop 65 standards, identifies remaining exposure, implements warning compliance across all sales channels, and builds the documentation system that prevents follow-on notices. Post-settlement audits start at $500 per SKU.

FDA Import Refusals

FDA Refusal Response — Admissibility Package & Import Alert Prevention

When FDA refuses a shipment your customs broker handles the CBP entry side. Moses Compliance handles the FDA side — the admissibility response, corrected labeling to meet 21 CFR requirements, reconditioning applications, private laboratory analysis coordination using US-based accredited facilities, and formal correspondence to the FDA District Office. We work alongside your customs broker so both sides of the refusal are covered simultaneously. For companies on FDA Import Alert: full Import Alert removal package including the required laboratory analysis and compliance documentation.

EU Safety Gate

EU Safety Gate Defense — GPSR Compliance & Amazon EU Reinstatement

EU Safety Gate listings trigger mandatory withdrawal across all 27 EU member states. Moses Compliance prepares the complete corrective action defense, coordinates with EU-based accredited laboratories for chemical and electrical safety testing, provides EU Responsible Person representation under GPSR 2023/988, and prepares the Amazon EU reinstatement documentation simultaneously. For multi-country cascade situations — listings appearing in two or more member states — we coordinate a unified response across all notifying authorities in one engagement. UK Responsible Person services available simultaneously for companies selling in both EU and UK markets.

Lab Coordination

Accredited Laboratory Coordination — Safe Harbor Testing & Product Certification

Moses Compliance manages the entire laboratory process on your behalf — we do not simply recommend a lab and leave you to manage it. We identify the correct ISO 17025 accredited facility for your specific product and chemical profile, prepare and submit samples, manage the testing timeline, review results against regulatory thresholds, and incorporate findings into your compliance documentation package. For Prop 65 safe harbor testing: lead, phthalates, cadmium, and other listed chemicals. For CPSC certification: ASTM F963, 16 CFR, and other applicable standards. For EU testing: EN standards and REACH chemical compliance. Lab coordination fee separate from laboratory costs.

Ongoing Program

Ongoing Regulatory Compliance Program — Monthly Monitoring & Catalog Protection

For companies that want continuous protection rather than reactive crisis management. Monthly monitoring across Prop 65, CPSC, FDA, EU Safety Gate, and Amazon enforcement channels. Immediate alert when your brand or product category is flagged. Quarterly compliance audit of your product catalog against current standards. Warning label review and update as regulations change. Supplier documentation system maintenance. Ongoing Amazon listing compliance monitoring. Retainer programs start at $400 per month for small catalogs. This is the service that ensures a Prop 65 settlement never leads to a second Prop 65 settlement.
