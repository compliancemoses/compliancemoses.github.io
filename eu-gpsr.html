The Situation

Selling in the EU
Just Got More Complex.

The EU General Product Safety Regulation (GPSR), which took full effect in December 2024, requires all products sold in the European Union to have an EU Responsible Person — a legal entity physically located within the EU that assumes regulatory responsibility for the product. For US sellers and exporters without an EU presence, this is now a mandatory compliance requirement. Non-compliance means products cannot legally enter EU markets.

What we know: GPSR sits alongside REACH, CBAM, and CE marking requirements as part of the EU's expanding product compliance framework. US sellers who understand only one layer of this system are exposed on the others. Moses Compliance manages the complete EU compliance picture — not just one regulation at a time.

Questions

What Clients Ask First

What exactly is an EU Responsible Person and why do I need one?
Under the EU GPSR, every product sold in the EU must have an EU Responsible Person — a legal entity based within the EU whose contact information appears on the product. This entity accepts legal accountability for the product's compliance with EU safety requirements. US companies without an EU legal presence must designate a third-party EU Responsible Person. Moses Compliance provides this service.
I already sell on Amazon EU — does GPSR affect my listings?
Yes. Amazon has implemented GPSR compliance requirements across all EU marketplaces. Sellers without an EU Responsible Person designated in their Amazon account face listing suppression. Moses Compliance provides EU Responsible Person services that satisfy both the regulation and Amazon's marketplace requirements.
What is CBAM and does it apply to my products?
The Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism applies to imports into the EU of carbon-intensive goods — currently including steel, aluminum, cement, fertilizers, electricity, and hydrogen. If you export these categories into the EU, CBAM reporting and eventually financial obligations apply. We assess applicability and manage the reporting process.
We export from China to EU — not from the US. Do we still need this?
Yes. GPSR and EU Responsible Person requirements apply based on where the product is sold — the EU market — not where it is manufactured or shipped from. Chinese exporters selling directly into EU markets need the same EU Responsible Person designation as US exporters.
