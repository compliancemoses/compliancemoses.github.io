The Situation

Amazon Enforcement
Moves Without Warning.

Amazon's compliance enforcement is automated, aggressive, and deliberately opaque. Suspension notices cite regulations without specifying exactly what documentation is required. Sellers who submit the wrong evidence — or submit it through the wrong channel — lose days, then weeks, while revenue stops entirely.

What we know: Amazon's compliance requirements by product category, violation type, and regulatory basis are specific and non-negotiable. The documentation that clears a CPSC violation is not the same as what clears an FCC issue. Category by category, we know what works and what Amazon's compliance team rejects.

Questions

What Clients Ask First

Why does Amazon keep rejecting my compliance documentation?
Amazon's compliance requirements are product-category specific and change without public announcement. A test report that satisfies one category may not satisfy another. The format, scope, accreditation of the testing laboratory, and specific chemicals tested all matter. We know the current requirements by category.
How long does reinstatement take?
When the correct documentation is submitted through the correct channel, Amazon typically responds within 3 to 7 business days. The delay is almost always caused by incorrect or incomplete submissions, not by Amazon's review timeline. A complete, correct first submission is everything.
My whole account is at risk — not just one listing.
Account-level compliance violations are more serious than single listing suspensions. We treat these as high-priority matters and audit your entire product catalog to identify and address all exposure simultaneously — not one listing at a time.
I sell on Amazon EU as well — can you help with both?
Yes. Amazon EU enforces EU GPSR, REACH, and CE marking requirements independently from Amazon US. We handle both simultaneously where clients sell across both marketplaces.
